About Me

My passion for computer science manifested when I found Google Chrome’s ‘Inspect Element’ function, which allows you to see a site’s code. I found this fascinating, and was mesmerized by the thousands of lines of code that make a website work. That night, I raced to my computer and played around with the code. I immediately began to experiment and teach myself, watching countless tutorials. I coded my own websites and programs. My programming journey has led me to many unique languages. I also love to share my passion with others; I lead my school’s computer science club, where I introduce members to the wondrous capabilities of programming. I have always been amazed by the practical implementations of programming, such as understanding data and turning ideas into apps.
